# Process 进度条
显示数据的进度加载

## 基本使用
进度条通过type属性设置条形加载条和环形加载条

<Playground>
  <div style={{'margin': '10px'}}>
    <Process type="line" percentage="25" />
    <Process type="line" percentage="50" />
    <Process type="line" percentage="75" />
    <Process type="line" percentage="100" />
  </div>
  <div style={{'margin': '10px'}}>
    <Process type="circle" percentage="25" />
    <Process type="circle" percentage="50" />
    <Process type="circle" percentage="75" />
    <Process type="circle" percentage="100" />
  </div>
</Playground>

## 颜色状态
进度条通过status属性显示颜色进度

<Playground>
  <div style={{'margin': '10px'}}>
    <Process type="line" percentage="25" />
    <Process type="line" percentage="75" status="exception" />
    <Process type="line" percentage="100" status="success" />
  </div>
  <div style={{'margin': '10px'}}>
    <Process type="circle" percentage="25" />
    <Process type="circle" percentage="75" status="exception"/>
    <Process type="circle" percentage="100" status="success" />
  </div>
</Playground>

## 文本显示
进度条通过showText属性显示额外文本
<Playground>
  <div style={{'margin': '10px'}}>
    <Process type="line" percentage="100" showText="100" />
    <Process type="line" percentage="100" showText="100" strokeWidth="10" textInside />
  </div>
  <div style={{'margin': '10px'}}>
    <Process type="circle" percentage="100" showText="100" />
  </div>
</Playground>

## 属性

属性名|名称|类型|默认值
----|---|---|---
percentage|进度条百分比|number|0
type|进度条类型|['line', 'circle']|'line'
strokeWidth|进度条高度|number|6
textInside|文本是否显示在进度条内部(仅在横向进度条使用)|boolean|false
showText|进度条是否显示文本|boolean|true
status|进度条颜色状态|string|-
width|进度条宽度|number|126
